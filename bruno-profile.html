Bruno

Name: Bruno
Age: 9 months
Sex: Male
Breed: English Bulldog
Temperament: Lazy
Location: Waterford

Introducing Bruno, an adorable 9-month-old English Bulldog puppy located in Waterford. Despite his young age, Bruno has already perfected the art of relaxation and is known for his laid-back and leisurely lifestyle. As a growing English Bulldog, Bruno embraces his lazy nature with gusto. He loves nothing more than curling up in his cozy bed or lounging on the couch, enjoying long afternoon naps. With his squishy face and droopy eyes, Bruno has mastered the art of looking utterly content and relaxed.
