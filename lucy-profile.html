Lucy

Name: Lucy
Age: 2 years
Sex: Female
Breed: Cairn Terrier
Temperament: Friendly

Lucy's friendly disposition extends not only to humans but also to other furry companions. She is alway ready to embrace any opportunity to engage in playful interactions with dogs of all shapes and sizes.
