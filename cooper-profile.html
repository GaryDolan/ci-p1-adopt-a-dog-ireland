Cooper

Name: Cooper
Age: 6 years
Sex: Male
Breed: German longhaired pointer
Temperament: Mellow
Location: Kildare

Meet Cooper, a handsome 6-year-old male German Longhaired Pointer located in Kildare. Cooper is known for his mellow temperament, which sets him apart as a serene and easygoing companion. With his calm and composed nature, Cooper brings a sense of tranquility to any environment he enters. Cooper's laid-back demeanor makes him an ideal partner for those seeking a relaxed and peaceful dog. Whether you're enjoying a leisurely stroll through the countryside or unwinding in the comfort of your home, Cooper's mellow disposition ensures a serene and harmonious experience.
