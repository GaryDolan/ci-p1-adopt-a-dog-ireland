Daisy

Name: Daisy
Age: 1 years
Sex: Female
Breed: Australian shepherd
Temperament: Intelligent
Location: Cork

Daisy, the one-year-old Australian Shepherd located in Cork, is a shining example of intelligence in the canine world. With her sharp mind and quick thinking, she's always up for a mental challenge. Daisy's intelligence shines through in her ability to learn new commands and tricks with ease. Whether it's mastering complex agility courses or solving puzzle toys, she thrives on mental stimulation and is sure to impress with her problem-solving skills. Daisy is also very adaptability and effortlessly adjusts to new situations and environments, making her an excellent companion for those with an active lifestyle.
