Name: Toby
Age: 3 years
Sex: Male
Breed: Boxer mixed breed
Temperament: Protective
Location: Kilkenny

Meet Toby, a loyal and protective 3-year-old Boxer mixed breed located in Kilkenny. Toby possesses a natural instinct to safeguard his loved ones, making him an excellent choice for those seeking a reliable and vigilant guard dog. With his strong and muscular physique, Toby exudes an aura of strength and presence that demands respect. While Toby's protective nature shines through, it's important to note that he is not an aggressive dog by nature. His watchful demeanor and instinctual protective tendencies are balanced with a calm and gentle disposition
