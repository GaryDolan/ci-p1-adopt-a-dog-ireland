About Us

Adopt a Dog Ireland is a family owned and operated dog shelter. It is owned and operated by the Dolan family. The main reason we started our shelter was because we love Dogs, always have, always will. We establish Adopt-a-Dog Ireland in 2023 in the efforts to help house as many dogs as possible. We wanted to make a shelter and accompanying website to make the process of adopting a dog fun and simple. We pride ourselves on the ability to find the perfect dog to match your lifestyle. At Adopt-a-Dog Ireland we try our utmost to ensure that the process of dog adoption is as effortless as possible. We want you to enjoy every stage of the process and for you to get your new best friend home with no stress involved.

Meet the Team

The Dolan's

Meet the Dolan family, Gary, Amanda and Jake. As a family of dedicated dog lovers, we have always welcomed dogs into our home throughout every stage of our lives. Gary, with his passion for technology and web development, takes charge of developing and maintaining our website. His expertise ensures that our online presence is informative, user-friendly, and accessible to all who are interested in adopting a furry friend. Amanda oversees the day-to-day operations of our shelter. She provides a safe and loving environment for each dog in our care, ensuring their physical and emotional well-being. Jake puts his stamp on every dog that passes through the shelter and really enjoys spending time training the dogs.

Blue and Buddy

Blue and Buddy are also an integral part of the team and were both adopted from shelters. They spend a lot of time with the dogs at the shelter and as two extremely friendly, fun loving goofballs and really help our dogs acclimate to their temporary home while there are being matched with their new owners.
